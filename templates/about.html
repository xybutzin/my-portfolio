Professional Bio

Blending biology with technology to solve complex research challenges.

As a PhD-trained microbiologist with a passion for technology, I combine my deep understanding of biological systems with the power of programming to solve complex problems in scientific research. I have honed my skills through online learning and hands-on projects, including machine-learning, data visulization, and web development. My technical toolkit extends beyond Python, encompassing a versatile mix of tools and methodologies that allow me to solve interdisciplinary problems with creativity and rigor.

By bridging my scientific expertise with programming and computational methods, I am committed to advancing research and developing solutions that have a meaningful impact in both the scientific community and beyond.
